Want to know a secret?!

cheek 2 cheek!? what is that!?

Cheek 2 Cheek was established in July 2015 by myself, Jessica Okwuonu.
At first the aim was to change the world and make everyone smile but it became very clear, very quickly that this was TOO BIG of an idea. So we started doing smaller things, trying to make the students and staff at De Monfort University feel special about themselves and happy to be a part of our amazing university. In the future we hope to do a bit more! ...
This page is currently under construction and will be updated soon!
For now enjoy what we have so far.
Have a good and happy day!
Jessica, Cheek 2 Cheek Founder.
